# Bug Investigation Template

## Observed Behavior

What is happening now?

## Expected Behavior

What should happen instead?

## Likely Area of Impact

Which files, modules, flows, or services are most likely involved?

## Suspected Cause

What seems most likely to be wrong based on the current code?

## Minimal Fix Strategy

What is the smallest change likely to solve the issue safely?

## Verification Plan

How will the fix be confirmed?

- regression test
- targeted manual reproduction
- logs / instrumentation
- integration verification

## Risks

What could break or need extra attention?
